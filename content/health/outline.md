## Routine Vet Visit Frequency

[Content Writer note: Puppy (monthly until 16 weeks), adult (annually), senior 7+ (biannually). What to expect at each visit: dental assessment, heartworm test, parasite prevention, weight check.]

## Parasite Prevention

[Content Writer note: Heartworm (monthly prevention year-round), flea/tick (topical or oral options), intestinal worms (routine deworming). Stress vet guidance over OTC products.]

## Emergency Warning Signs

[Content Writer note: Present as a bold, scannable list. When to go to an emergency vet immediately:

- Difficulty breathing
- Bloated/distended abdomen
- Seizure lasting more than 5 minutes or cluster seizures
- Sudden hind-limb weakness or paralysis
- Pale/white gums
- Collapse or unresponsiveness
- Suspected poisoning (cross-link to /diet-nutrition foods-to-avoid section)]
