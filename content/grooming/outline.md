## Bathing

### How Often to Bathe a Beagle

[Content Writer note: Every 4–6 weeks is typical, or as needed. Over-bathing strips natural oils. Beagles can have a "hound smell" — explain cause and management.]

### Bathing Technique

[Content Writer note: Step-by-step: wet coat thoroughly, apply dog shampoo, lather, rinse fully (residue causes skin irritation), towel dry then air or blow dry on low. Use dog-formulated shampoo only.]

### Shampoo Selection

[Content Writer note: What to look for — pH-balanced for dogs, no harsh chemicals. Mention oatmeal formulas for sensitive skin. Avoid human shampoos.]
